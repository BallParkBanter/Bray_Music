# Juggernaut XL: A Master's Guide to Photorealistic AI Imagery

Source: https://www.rundiffusion.com/juggernaut-xl-rundiffusion-guide

## Overview
Juggernaut XL is a custom-trained model built on SDXL architecture by KandooAI/RunDiffusion. Excels at photorealistic images with cinematic quality and rich detail in textures (skin, fabric, environments).

## Prompt Structure Formula

Build prompts in layers: (Subject), (Action/Pose), (Environment/Setting), (Style), (Lighting), (Technical Details)

**Example:** "A majestic lion standing proudly on a rocky outcrop with Serengeti background, fantasy realism style, dramatic cinematic lighting, high resolution with detailed fur texture."

## Recommended Settings
- Sampler: DPM++ 2M Karras
- Steps: 30
- CFG Scale: 5 (range 4-6 optimal)

## Negative Prompt
"(deformed, distorted, disfigured), poorly drawn, bad anatomy, wrong anatomy, extra limb, missing limb, floating limbs, (mutated hands and fingers), disconnected limbs, mutation, mutated, ugly, disgusting, blurry, amputation, cgi, 3d, digital, airbrushed"

## Creative Examples

### Hyper-Realistic Portrait
"Close up portrait of an elderly man with weathered face, deep wrinkles, and gentle smile. A single tear rolls down his cheek. Dramatic, emotional lighting, photorealistic, 8k, sharp focus, detailed skin texture."

### Epic Sci-Fi
"Cinematic still of futuristic cyberpunk city street at night, neon signs reflecting in puddles, lone figure in trench coat walking away, blade runner style, volumetric lighting, ultra-detailed."

### High-Fantasy
"Fierce warrior cat with (glowing enchanted:1.2) axe charging into battle with dynamic powerful movement, wearing ornate battle-worn armor, fur bristling, intense gaze, fantasy realism style, high resolution, detailed metallic textures."

## Non-Photorealistic Capabilities
Despite its photorealism focus, Juggernaut XL can handle: oil painting, watercolor, anime, surrealism, and gothic horror styles.
