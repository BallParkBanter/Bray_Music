# Prompt Guide for Juggernaut XI and XII

Source: https://learn.rundiffusion.com/prompt-guide-for-juggernaut-xi-and-xii/

## Overview
Juggernaut XI and XII are improvements over Juggernaut X. They accept both detailed and simpler prompts while delivering quality results.

## Model Settings

### Juggernaut XI
- Sampling Method: DPM++ 2M Karras
- Sampling Steps: 25-40
- CFG: 4-6
- Resolution: 1024x1024, 832x1216, 1216x832
- Negative Prompt: "Naked, Nude, fake eyes, deformed eyes, bad eyes, cgi, 3D, digital, airbrushed"

### Juggernaut XII
- Sampling Methods: 3M SDE Exponential, DPM++ 2M Karras, Euler
- CFG: 4-6
- Negative Prompt: "Naked, Nude, bad hands, deformed eyes, bad eyes, cgi, 3D, digital, airbrushed"
- **Superior style versatility** compared to XI

## Key Prompting Principles

1. **First Sentence Priority** — Opening language sets the foundation
2. **Traditional Styles Supported** — Tags, sentences, and conventional methods all work
3. **Weight Application** — Use (term:1.2) syntax judiciously
4. **Token Constraints** — Under 75 tokens for best adherence
5. **Trigger Words** — "High Resolution," "Cinematic," "Skin Textures"

## Artistic Medium Examples (Juggernaut XII Strength)

**Watercolor:**
"(watercolor:1.2) painting of (tranquil lake:1.1) at sunset, soft brushstrokes capturing serene atmosphere, subtle colors blending together"

**Oil Painting:**
"(oil:1.2) painting of (Clown:1.1) with large expressive eyes, bold brushstrokes capturing sadness, vibrant colors"

**Surrealism:**
"(Surrealism:1.3) A dreamlike landscape with floating islands, twisted trees, and impossible architecture, vibrant colors, soft lighting"

**Japanese Ink:**
"Style (Japanese ink painting:1.2) landscape of (serene mountain village:1.3), in style of (Sesshū Tōyō:1.3), with delicate brushstrokes, monochromatic palette"

**Abstract:**
"Style (Abstract:1.2) depiction of (woman's silhouette:1.3) with (dripping paint:1.2) blending vibrant colors, thick layers of paint and smooth drips"

- **Soft Diffused:** "Gentle transitions between light and shadow, creating subtle and serene depth, cool tones"

## Model Selection
- **Juggernaut XI** — Optimal for portraits and balanced detail
